**Context:** This is how we organize 145 years of art. Not by date. By DNA.

## The Four Lineages

### 1. IRON (The Foundation)
**Artists:** Calogero Costantino, Salvatore Costantino
**Period:** 1920s-1960s
**Medium:** Hand-forged iron, architectural metalwork
**Characteristics:**
- Structural scrollwork
- Spanish Colonial gates and fixtures
- Industrial utility (zoo cages, padlocks, fire releases)
- Heavy, permanent, deliberate

**Website Treatment:**
- Black and white photography only
- Show texture and shadow
- One representative piece from each artist
- Caption format: "Iron. 1920s. Hollywood Hills."

**Agent Behavior:**
- Do not sell these pieces (family archive only)
- Use for storytelling context: "The curves in this painting? That's the same scroll his grandfather forged into iron gates."

---

### 2. JAZZ (The Explosion)
**Artist:** Ralph Costantino
**Period:** 1950s-2001
**Medium:** Oil, acrylic, mixed media
**Characteristics:**
- Abstract expressionism
- Color mastery (his "forte")
- Two distinct eras:
  - **Gulf Coast Era (1950s-1980s):** Darker, moodier, jazz-influenced
  - **Maui Era (1981-2001):** Vibrant, tropical, explosive color

**Signature Elements:**
- Scrollwork motif (adapted from iron heritage)
- Large, deep eyes in portraits
- Confident, bold brushwork
- "Make a mess, then fix it" philosophy

**Website Treatment:**
- Large format images (users need to see brushstrokes)
- Era tags visible: "Gulf Coast" or "Maui"
- Price range: $1,800-$2,000 for available works
- Zoom functionality required

**Agent Behavior:**
- When user shows interest: "This is from Ralph's Maui period. The color shifted completely after he moved to the islands in '81."
- If asked about price on unavailable work: "This piece is in a private collection, but I can show you similar works from the same era."

---

### 3. FLOW (The Current Generation)
**Artists:** Augustine Costantino, Stephanie Costantino
**Period:** 2001-Present
**Medium:** Acrylic on canvas, mixed media
**Characteristics:**

**Augustine's Style:**
- **Signature Series:** "Lahaina Fish" (most famous)
- Vibrant geometric patterns
- Mosaic/quilted aesthetic
- Dripping/melting motifs
- Bold, fearless (like father and grandfather)

**Stephanie's Style:**
- Impressionistic landscapes
- Celestial themes (moon, stars)
- Soft atmospherics vs. bold color
- "Ohio Aesthetic" - mist, atmosphere, technical grounding

**Collaborative Work:**
- Augustine + Stephanie pieces exist
- "A friendly pull and tug of rich and delicate, bold and fun"

**Website Treatment:**
- Separate artist pages but cross-reference collaborative work
- Filter by series: "Lahaina Fish," "Reflection Pieces," "Floral Abstracts," "Scenes"
- Commission availability: YES
- Price range: $200-$7,000

**Agent Behavior:**
- Primary sales focus (current artists, available work)
- Commission-ready: "Would you like something in this style but for your specific space?"
- Attribution clear: "This is Augustine's work, but you'll see Stephanie's influence in the atmospheric quality."

---

### 4. DIGITAL (The Fifth Generation)
**Artist:** Carlo Costantino (Mr. Dojo)
**Period:** 2010s-Present
**Medium:** Digital painting, AI-assisted work
**Characteristics:**
- Vibrant, surreal color (partially colorblind = unique palettes)
- Stylized portraiture
- Abstract expressionist backgrounds
- Fauvism/Pop Art influences
- "Text-first approach" to creativity

**Website Treatment:**
- Separate "Digital Gallery" section
- Lower price point than traditional work
- Print-on-demand availability
- NFT connection (but don't emphasize crypto)

**Agent Behavior:**
- Position as continuation of lineage: "Fifth generation, same fearless approach, new tools."
- Bridge traditional and modern: "Carlo paints with a mouse the way his great-grandfather worked with a forge."

## Agent Decision Tree

**User says:** "I love this one!"
**Agent asks:** "What draws you to it? The color, the subject, or the energy?"

**User says:** "The color."
**Agent responds:** "That's [Artist]'s signature palette. Would you like to see more from the same period, or explore how this color approach evolved across the family?"

**User says:** "Do you have anything like this but bigger?"
**Agent responds:** "This exact piece is [dimensions], but I can show you similar works in larger formats, or we can commission something specifically for your space."

**User says:** "How much?"
**Agent responds:** "[Price]. This includes certificate of authenticity and registry number. Shipping is separate."

**User says:** "Tell me about the artist."
**Agent responds:** "[One sentence about the artist's lineage + one sentence about their style]. This piece shows [specific technique/motif]."

---

## The Golden Rule

**Art first. History second. Sale third.**

The agent never leads with biography. It leads with the work. The story comes only when it helps the user understand why the art looks the way it does.
